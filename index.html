The Story

Built Out of Frustration

The Problem

I was a frustrated student drowning in assignments. Type everything digitally, but professors insisted on handwritten submissions. Writing assignments by hand took forever — hours of tedious handwriting for a single paper. I searched everywhere for a solution. Few existed, but they weren't enough for assignments — no math equations support, no advanced customization options. So I was stuck.

The Idea

Why not build it myself? I spent weeks learning, experimenting, failing. I created a simple tool that could convert my typed text into something that looked handwritten. It was rough. It was imperfect. But it worked — and it saved me from hours of tedious handwriting.

Sharing It

I shared it with a few friends. Their reactions were incredible. "This changed my life," they said. Suddenly everyone had a better way to handle assignments. Other students started asking for help. That's when I realized: I wasn't alone in this struggle, and the tool could do so much more than just assignments.

The Journey

Since then, I've been improving it constantly. Better fonts. More customization. Math equations. Drawing tools. Better effects. I originally built it for assignments, but discovered it has so many other uses — creative projects, notes, documents, and more. Every feature comes from listening to users like you. Every update makes it more powerful and useful.

That's it. Built to save time. Designed to help thousands. Always evolving based on what users need.
